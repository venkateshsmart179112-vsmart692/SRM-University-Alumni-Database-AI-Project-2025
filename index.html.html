SRM In Focus

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) is one of the top-ranking universities in India with over 50,000 students and more than 3,000 faculty across all its campuses. The University offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Medicine and Health Sciences, and Science and Humanities.
